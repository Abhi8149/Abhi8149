# 💫 About Me:
I'm Abhinav — a student of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at NIT Kurukshetra who doesn't just study intelligent systems but ships them. My backend runs on Node.js, my data lives in MySQL, and my algorithms speak fluent C++. I've built everything from collaborative document editors and anonymous messaging platforms to RAG-powered chat apps, GAN-based image generators, and an Ethereum phishing detection model. When I'm not designing scalable systems or dissecting LLDs of real-world products like Zomato, Spotify, and payment gateways, I'm pushing the frontier — building agentic AI pipelines with LangChain, MCP, and LLMs that can actually reason and act.<br>
